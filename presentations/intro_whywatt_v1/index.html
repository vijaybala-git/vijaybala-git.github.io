03 · The interface · 2 of 4

Set up your home

  • Home & Solar. ZIP, bedrooms, square footage, and California climate zone — plus an optional solar + battery add.
  • Energy & Prices. Model horizon (20 yrs) and rate models: electricity +7%/yr, gas +8%/yr, gasoline and EV-charging rates too.
  • Social & Health. Optional climate cost (CO₂ + methane, 1.07 $/therm) and air-quality cost (1.23 $/therm), plus gasoline externalities.
  • Sensible defaults. Everything is pre-filled for a Bay Area home — adjust only what you know.

Scenario 01

Replace-on-failure

The Technophile

Researches options and costs carefully, but isn't especially price-sensitive. Wants the right system — and wants to time it well.

  • Replaces on failure. Furnace and water heater are both near end-of-life — a swap is coming in under 3 years.
  • Heat-pump ready. Drawn to efficiency (COP 3.5, SEER 22) and the cleaner long-run trajectory.
  • Rebate-aware, not rebate-driven. Takes the $3,500 HVAC rebate, but it isn't the deciding factor.
